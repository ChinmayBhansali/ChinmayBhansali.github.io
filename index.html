About Me

Hello! I'm Chinmay

I'm an Integrated Sciences student at the University of British Columbia, where I blend my passion for Computer Science, Psychology, and Mathematics to explore the fascinating intersection of technology and human interaction.

I enjoy building intuitive software solutions, dynamic web applications, and engaging games.
